Top 5 Metrics Every Website Owner Should Track

  • Page Views: Which pages are getting the most attention?
  • Bounce Rate: How many visitors leave without interacting?
  • Session Duration: How long are users staying?
  • Traffic Sources: Where is your audience coming from?
  • Conversion Rate: How many visitors complete desired actions?
